# Arbeitly — Free Candidate Overview

---

## Who is a Free Candidate?

A free candidate is a job seeker who has registered on Arbeitly without purchasing a paid plan. They have access to a limited but functional set of tools to independently track and manage their job search.

Free candidates are **self-service users**. They are not assigned to an advisor and do not receive any agency-managed services such as CV preparation, cover letter writing, or interview coaching through the platform. They use Arbeitly purely as a personal job search organiser.

Free candidates represent the top of the funnel — the expectation is that a portion will convert to a paid plan once they experience the platform and understand the value of the full advisory service.

---

## How a Free Candidate Joins

1. Discovers Arbeitly via marketing, referral, or organic search
2. Clicks **Get Started Free** on the landing or pricing page
3. Completes registration — name, email, password
4. Answers a short **marketing questionnaire** — industry of interest, target country, and how they heard about Arbeitly
5. Lands on their personal portal

> The free onboarding is intentionally lightweight. No detailed career history is collected at this stage — that depth is reserved for paid candidates who will be actively worked with by an advisor.

## Board

The Board is the free candidate's home screen. It provides a high-level snapshot of where they stand across their job search at a glance.

It shows a summary of applications grouped by status:

- **To Apply** — jobs they intend to apply to
- **Applied** — applications submitted
- **Interview** — active interview processes
- **Accepted** — offers received
- **Rejected** — unsuccessful applications

The board gives a quick visual sense of pipeline health without requiring any extra input beyond what they have already entered in Applications.

---

## Applications

The Applications section is the core tool for free candidates. It is a personal job application tracker where they can log, manage, and monitor every position they are pursuing.

### Adding an Application

A candidate can manually add any job by filling in:

| Field | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Position Name | Yes | Job title being applied for |
| Company | Yes | Company name |
| Application Date | No | When they applied or plan to apply |
| Status | No | Current stage (defaults to To Apply) |
| Job URL | No | Link to the original job posting |
| Salary | No | Expected or advertised salary |
| Contact Person | No | Recruiter or hiring manager name |
| Next Action | No | Reminder of what to do next |
| Job Description | No | Paste or summarise the JD for reference |

### Managing Applications

- **Edit** any application they added at any time
- **Delete** any application they added
- **Change status** via a dropdown — moves the application through the pipeline
- **Search** across all entries by job title or company name
- **Filter** by status to focus on a specific stage

### Attribution

Every application added by a free candidate is tagged as self-added (`source: self`). If they later upgrade to a paid plan and are assigned an advisor, any applications the advisor adds will be clearly attributed — showing "Added by [Advisor Name]" — and cannot be edited or deleted by the candidate.

## CSV Import & Export

Free candidates can manage their application list in bulk.

### Download Template
Downloads a pre-formatted CSV file with the correct column headers. The candidate fills it in with their existing applications and uploads it.

### Import CSV
- Upload a filled CSV file
- The platform validates every row before committing — any missing required fields (job title or company) are flagged
- A preview is shown before confirmation — the candidate can review all rows
- If any rows have errors, the entire import is blocked until corrected (no partial imports)
- Successfully imported entries are added to the top of the application list

### Export CSV
- Downloads all current applications as a CSV file
- Filename is personalised with the candidate's name
- Useful for keeping a local backup or sharing progress externally

## Profile

The Profile section allows the free candidate to manage their account details.

They can update:
- First and last name
- Email address
- Password (requires current password verification)

> For free candidates, there is no career profile or onboarding data stored. The only personal data held is what they provided at registration plus their marketing questionnaire answers (industry, target location, how they heard about Arbeitly).

---

## What Free Candidates Do Not Get

The following are exclusive to paid candidates who are actively being managed by an advisor:

- **CV Library** — Advisor-built CVs in multiple versions and styles, downloadable as PDF
- **Cover Letters** — Tailored cover letters per job application
- **FAQ / Interview Prep** — Advisor-curated Q&A pairs for interview preparation, with the ability to approve or personalise answers
- **Job Discovery** — AI-powered job matching based on a detailed career profile
- **AI CV Enhancement** — AI rewrites and improves CV content
- **Onboarding Profile** — Detailed career questionnaire covering experience, education, skills, salary expectations, and goals
- **Advisor Assignment** — A dedicated advisor actively managing their placement

---

## Upgrading to a Paid Plan

A free candidate can upgrade at any time by visiting the **Upgrade** page from within their portal. Upgrading:

1. Unlocks the full candidate portal (Files, FAQ, Onboarding, Job Discovery)
2. Triggers an advisor assignment by the Super Admin
3. Initiates the full onboarding questionnaire to build a detailed career profile
4. Begins the active placement service

All applications and data already tracked on the free plan carry over seamlessly — nothing is lost on upgrade.
