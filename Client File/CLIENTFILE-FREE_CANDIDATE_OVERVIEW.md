\# Arbeitly – Candidate Journey (Free Plan)

\---

\#\# 1\. Overview

The Free Plan is the \*\*freemium acquisition layer\*\* of Arbeitly.

Its purpose is to:  
\- Provide immediate value to candidates  
\- Help them organize their job search  
\- Introduce core workflows (CV \+ Applications)  
\- Drive conversion to Paid Plans

\---

\#\# 2\. Entry Point

Candidate lands on:  
→ Pricing Page

Selects:  
→ Free Plan

\---

\#\# 3\. Account Creation Flow

\---

\#\#\# 3.1 Signup

Fields:  
\- Full Name  
\- Email  
\- Password

\---

\#\#\# 3.2 Marketing Questions (Onboarding)

Purpose:  
\- User segmentation  
\- Marketing insights  
\- Funnel optimization

\---

Fields (Dropdown-based):

\- Are you currently a German resident?  
\- Where did you hear about Arbeitly?  
\- How are you currently managing your job search?

→ Candidate Profile (Marketing Data Section)

\---

\#\#\# 5.1 Entry State

\- Upload CV    
\- OR Create CV from scratch

\#\#\# 5.2 CV Upload & Parsing

\- Extracts structured data  
\- Populates internal editor

\---

\#\#\# 5.3 CV Editor

  \- Experience  
  \- Education  
  \- Skills

\#\#\# 5.4 Templates

\- Template B

\#\#\# 5.5 Export Flow

2\. Names file

\#\#\# 5.7 Storage Logic
